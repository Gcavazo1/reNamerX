Features

reNamerX is packed with powerful features designed to make batch file renaming fast, easy, and precise.

User Interface

Modern Design

Clean, intuitive interface with dark and cyberpunk themes that reduce eye strain during extended sessions.

  • Responsive layout that adapts to different window sizes
  • Dark and cyberpunk themes to match your preference
  • Drag-and-drop support for adding files

File Browser

Powerful file management capabilities to help you select and organize your files.

  • Browse and select individual files or entire folders
  • Filter files by name, extension, or other criteria
  • Sort files by name, date, size, or type

Renaming Operations

Text Operations

Comprehensive text manipulation tools to transform your filenames exactly how you need them.

  • Find and replace text within filenames
  • Add prefixes or suffixes to filenames
  • Remove specific characters or patterns
  • Insert text at specific positions

Case Transformations

Convert the case of your filenames to match your preferred style and organization system.

  • Convert to UPPERCASE or lowercase
  • Convert to Title Case or Sentence case
  • Transform to camelCase or PascalCase
  • Change to snake_case or kebab-case

Numbering

Add sequential numbers to organize files in a specific order, perfect for photo collections and documents.

  • Configure start number, increment, and digit padding
  • Place numbers at the beginning, end, or specific position
  • Include leading zeros (001, 002, etc.)
  • Number files across folders with continuous sequence

Advanced Features

Regular Expressions

Harness the power of regex for complex pattern matching and replacement operations.

  • Use regex for advanced pattern matching
  • Access captured groups in replacements
  • Full regex support with look-ahead and look-behind assertions

Preview & Undo

Work confidently with real-time feedback and the ability to revert any changes.

  • Real-time preview of rename operations
  • Side-by-side view of original and new filenames
  • Multi-level undo/redo support
  • History of recent operations

Presets & Batch Processing

Save time by reusing common operations and processing large numbers of files at once.

  • Save frequently used rename rules as presets
  • Apply multiple rules with a single click
  • Process hundreds or thousands of files at once
  • Handle large batches efficiently
